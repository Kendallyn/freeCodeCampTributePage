John Muir

"Father of the National Parks"

"In every walk with nature one receives far more than he seeks." -John Muir

Timeline

  1. 1838 - Born in Scotland
  2. 1868 - First visit to Yosemite Valley
  3. 1871 - Authored first Environmental essay printed in the New York Tribune
  4. 1890 - Authored "Features of the Proposed Yosemite National Park"
  5. 1892 - Cofounded the Sierra Club
  6. 1903 - John Muir and President Roosevelt travel to Yosemite, where Muir urged President Roosevelt to move Yosemite from state to federal protections
  7. 1911 - Documented wildlife experiences in California
  8. 1914 - Died in California
  9. 2006 - Inducted into the "California Hall of Fame" posthumously
